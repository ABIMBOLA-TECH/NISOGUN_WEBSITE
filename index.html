WELCOME TO NIS OGUN STATE BRANCH

NIS OGUN STATE BRANCH is committed to building a strong, prosperous, and inclusive society, foster a culture of innovation, excellence, and ethical leadership in our state and contribute to the development of the country and its people.

Surv Abiodun Liadi Olufemi, FNIS.

Chairman Foreword

Nigeria is a country that is growing rapidly and is poised to become a major player in the global economy. As we look to the future, we must ensure that our state remains a strong, prosperous, and inclusive society. Our mission is to foster a culture of innovation, excellence, and ethical leadership in our state and to contribute to the development of the country and its people. By working together, we will create a brighter future for ourselves and for all of Nigeria.

Nigeria is a landlocked country in West Africa, bordered by the Niger, Chad, Cameroon, and Togo. It has a population of approximately 21 million people, making it the 21st-largest country in the world. Nigeria is a fertile, diverse, and peaceful nation, with a rich history and a rich cultural heritage. The country is also a major exporter of agricultural products, fish, and timber, as well as a major producer of various industrial products, including steel, cement, and fertilizers.
