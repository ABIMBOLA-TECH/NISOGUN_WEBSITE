NIS CONSTITUTION

Our Constitution

The Nigerian Institution of Surveyors, Ogun State Branch (NISOGUN) is a non-profit organization dedicated to the promotion and development of surveying professionals in Nigeria. Established in 1998, NISOGUN has been committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive workforce by providing opportunities for individuals to gain knowledge, skills, and experience in the field of surveying.
